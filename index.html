Privacy Policy

1. Data We Collect

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Here is a detailed list of the data we collect:

  • IP Address: Used to monitor and maintain system security.
  • Usage Data: Includes information about how you use the app, such as session durations, pages visited, and actions performed.
  • Username: Collected during account registration for identification purposes.
  • Password: Stored securely in an encrypted format for account authentication.
  • Role: Identifies your user type (e.g., student or teacher) to personalize your experience.
  • Study Entries: Includes information about your study habits, progress, and activities recorded in the app.
  • ChatGPT Integration: We use ChatGPT to provide enhanced features and assistance. ChatGPT can access entry data and usernames to deliver these services.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to provide and improve our services, including:

  • Ensuring app security and preventing unauthorized access.
  • Tailoring the app experience based on your role.
  • Tracking your progress to help improve your study habits.
  • Generating anonymized data insights for app improvement.
  • Utilizing ChatGPT to enhance user assistance and provide insights based on entry data.

3. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with services. Upon account deletion, we will securely erase your data unless required for legal or compliance purposes.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. However, we may share data with trusted third-party service providers to support our services. These providers are contractually obligated to handle your data securely and for authorized purposes only.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and review your data.
  • Request correction of inaccuracies in your data.
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal and compliance requirements).
  • Withdraw consent for data processing.

6. Security Measures

We implement strict security measures to protect your data, including encryption, access control, and regular audits.

7. Updates to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. We will notify you of significant changes and encourage you to review the policy regularly.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@learnalyze.space.
